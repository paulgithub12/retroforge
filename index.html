Introduction

Food photography uses design elements to make dishes look appealing. Photographers use line, shape, colour, texture and space to create beautiful and engaging images of food.

Line

Lines guide the viewer's eye through an image. In food photography, lines can come from utensils, table edges, or food arrangement.

Shape

Shapes are created by the outlines of food items. Plates, bowls, and food portions create visual shapes in food photography.
