Line

Lines guide the viewer’s eye and create structure in a design.

Shape

Shapes are created when lines connect, such as circles, squares, and triangles.

Colour

Colour creates emotion and meaning in design and helps attract attention.

Texture

Texture describes how something might feel or appear visually.

Space

Space is the area around elements in a design and helps improve readability.
